Our Certifications

ISO9001

Our Quality Management System Certification ensures that we meet international standards for quality and consistency in our products and services.

ISPM#15

This certification ensures that our wood packaging materials are treated to prevent the spread of pests during international shipping.

CFIA/CWPCA

Regular inspections under this program ensure compliance with heat treatment regulations for wood packaging.

CWPCP

This program certifies that our wood packaging meets Canadian standards for safety and quality.
