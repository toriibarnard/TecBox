About Us

For over 28 years we have been designing and manufacturing customized shipping crates and boxes in all sizes and forms for the manufacturing industry. Our emphasis is on making sure your product arrives at its destination in the same pristine condition in which it departed.

With our strong service approach, guaranteed on-time delivery and focus on total customer satisfaction, we provide you with innovative products at an affordable price.

TecBox is equally committed to excellence in all aspects of our organization. Our company is ISO 9001 certified - a measure of our dedication to quality. By embracing quality in all areas of business, TecBox clients benefit through our efficient manufacturing processes and systems designed to ensure that customer requirements remain at the forefront of all that we do.

A focus on quality and a philosophy of continuous improvement… that’s the TecBox advantage!

We make regular deliveries throughout Halifax and Lunenburg Counties.
