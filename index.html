Custom Crate Design

Tell us the exact size of the item you'd like to ship, and we'll design a crate around it. We also ensure appropriate weight support and protective lining to keep fragile items safe.

Our Production

All our crates and boxes are manufactured in-house in Nova Scotia, using sustainable materials sourced locally. We ensure quality by using the strongest materials such as plywood, OSB, double-wall cardboard, and heat-treated lumber.

Shipping & Stocking Services

For customers with ongoing demand, we offer stocking services for immediate shipping. We are certified under multiple international standards to guarantee quality and compliance.

Special Services
