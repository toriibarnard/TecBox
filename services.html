Our Services

At TecBox International LTD, we provide a wide range of packaging solutions tailored to your needs. From custom crates to sustainable materials, we ensure your products are protected and delivered safely.

Design

Tell us the exact size of the item that you'd like to ship - we'll design a crate around it. These dimensions are called the 'inside dimensions'.

Tell us the weight of the item - we'll build a suitable base to support it.

Tell us how valuable or fragile it is - we'll line the crate with the appropriate grade of foam and if necessary, support the item with saddles produced to the exact specifications with our CNC Router.

Production

Our crates & boxes are manufactured in-house here in Nova Scotia.

At TecBox, we believe in sustainable business practices. From ensuring that our source materials are from qualified and certified suppliers who have also adopted sustainable practices to supporting the local economy. By sourcing our lumber, sheet materials, and cardboard locally, we are better able to control quality and cost efficiencies which enhance our value to our customers. Whenever possible, all our source materials are manufactured in Canada.

We use the strongest materials for your shipping crate or box, such as Plywood, OSB, Double wall Cardboard, and export certified, heat treated lumber (HT).

Shipping

For customers with ongoing demand, based on forecast or time-critical shipping requirements, we offer stocking services for immediate shipping.

We are certified:

  • Canadian Wood Products Certification Program (CWPCP)
  • ISPM#15 compliant
  • CFIA (Canadian Food Inspection Agency) certification
  • Regularly inspected by CWPCA (Canadian Wood Pellet & Container Association)

Special Services

We produce a variety of shop aids for the manufacturing industry. Jigs produced on our CNC Router for composite layup for aerospace parts. Specialized transport cases for movement between divisions in the production process.

Cardboard

Our patented cardboard crating system has a range of key advantages over other packaging systems in the market:

  • Cost-effective in low quantity runs
  • Crush-proof Double wall cardboard
  • Can be produced with a pallet base for pallet jack and forklift use
  • Used in the aerospace industry in the US, Canada & Europe
  • US Department of Defence (DoD) approved
